# Airbnb-Global-Performance-Dashboard
**Project Objective**
To provide stakeholders with a comprehensive view of Airbnb's global performance, enabling data-driven decisions related to market expansion, pricing strategy, host management, and customer experience optimization.
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Airbnb's global performance across major cities, property types, hosts, listings, pricing, and customer reviews. This project transforms raw Airbnb data into actionable insights that help understand market trends, host performance, and customer satisfaction.
**key Executives:**
Total Listings: 279,712
Cities Analyzed: 10
Hosts: 182,024
Property Types: 144
Reviews: 5.37M+
**Market & Growth Analysis**
Tracked Airbnb listing growth from 2008–2021
Identified key business lifecycle stages:
Introduction
Growth
Maturity
Reinvention
COVID-19 Impact
Highlighted peak listing growth and market decline periods
**Business Insights**
Airbnb experienced rapid growth until reaching market maturity before facing regulatory challenges and pandemic-related declines.
A small number of cities contribute the majority of listings, indicating strong market concentration.
Hotel rooms command the highest average prices, while private rooms remain the most affordable option.
Customer ratings remain consistently high across cities, reflecting strong user satisfaction and platform trust.
